Madagascar Movie

Madagascar is a 2005 American computer-animated comedy film produced by DreamWorks Animation, and released in movie theaters on May 27, 2005

Related Information
  • Initial release: May 25, 2005
  • Directors: Eric Darnell, Tom McGrath
  • Music composed by: Hans Zimmer
  • Characters: Julien, Melman, Alex, Mort, Gloria, Skipper, Kowalski
